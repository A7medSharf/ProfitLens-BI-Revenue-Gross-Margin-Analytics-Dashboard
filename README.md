# 📊 ProfitLens BI – Revenue & Gross Margin Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
ProfitLens BI is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze revenue performance, gross margin trends, supplier profitability, and team-based sales distribution.

The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into strategic financial insights that support operational and executive decision-making.

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

- Total Revenue: $9.68M
- Gross Margin: $4.01M
- Gross Margin Percentage: 41.5%
- Best Performing Month Identification
- Supplier Profitability Tracking

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Power Query
- Data Modeling
- Excel Dataset

---

## ⚙️ Data Preparation & Modeling
- Cleaned and transformed raw data using Power Query
- Built a structured data model
- Created calculated measures for Revenue, Gross Margin, and Margin %
- Implemented interactive filters for:
  - Team
  - Supervisor
  - Salesperson
  - Year / Quarter / Month

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Revenue Performance Analysis
- Monthly revenue trend tracking
- Identification of peak-performing months
- Sales growth visualization

### 2️⃣ Gross Margin Analysis
- Gross profit monitoring
- Margin percentage trend analysis
- Profitability insights by time period

### 3️⃣ Supplier Profitability
- Revenue contribution by supplier
- Gross margin comparison across suppliers
- Supplier performance ranking

### 4️⃣ Sales Team Performance
- Revenue breakdown by team
- Category-based revenue distribution
- Retail vs Wholesale vs Online channel analysis

## 📊 Business Insights Generated
- Identified most profitable suppliers
- Highlighted months with highest revenue and margin
- Revealed sales channel contribution to total revenue
- Supported data-driven pricing and supplier strategy
